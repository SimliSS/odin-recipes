Marinated Turkey Breast

Mouth-watering marinated turkey breast!

We will be showing you how easy it is to make the best and juiciest oven-roasted turkey breast recipe ever. Marinated with a mixture of maple syrup, lemon juice, garlic, and herbs. Enjoy tender, juicy, roasted turkey breast for Thanksgiving dinner without having to cook a whole turkey!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Boneless turkey breast (2-4 lbs)
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Tbsp Maple syrup or honey
  • 3 Tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice
  • 4 Garlic Cloves minced
  • 2 Tsp Chili Flakes
  • 2 Tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 Tsp Freshly ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Rinse the turkey breast and pat dry. (you may skip rinsing and just pat dry)
  2. In a small bowl combine all marinade ingredients.
  3. Place the turkey breast into a gallon ziplock together with the marinade, and mix well through the bag. For best results refrigerate overnight to marinate.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Transfer the turkey breast to an ovenproof dish or roasting tray and keep it on the counter for about 20 minutes, to get close to room temp.
  5. Place it in the preheated oven and roast until the juices run clear when pierced with a fork and a thermometer inserted into the thickest part of turkey registers 170°F, about 60minutes.
  6. Allow it to rest, covered with foil, for 10 minutes before carving.
