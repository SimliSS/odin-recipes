Crazy Cake

Delicious chocolate crazy cake!

Crazy cake is an easy homemade cake recipe that became popular during the depression era. It has no eggs, no milk and no butter. And to make it even simpler, you mix the cake ingredients right in the baking pan . That's right…no extra dishes!

Ingredients:

  • Flour.
  • Sugar. Only white sugar.
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • Baking soda.
  • Mini chocolate chips. optional.
  • White vinegar.
  • Vegetable oil
  • Water.

How To Make Crazy Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Place flour, sugar, cocoa powder, salt, baking soda, and chocolate chips into an ungreased 8*8 inch pan.
  3. Stir all the dry ingredients together and make a well in the center of the pan.
  4. Pour vanilla, vinegar, vegetable oil, and water into well and whisk together until just combined. Do not overmix. It's okay to have some lumps.
  5. Place in oven and bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool cake completely.

Ingredients for Chocolate Buttercream Frosting:

  • butter
  • powdered sugar
  • cocoa powder
  • salt
  • vanilla
  • heavy cream

Instructions for Chocolate Buttercream Frosting

  1. You'll whip the butter for about 1 ½ minutes until it is creamy and whitish in color.
  2. Then you'll add the salt and vanilla.
  3. And then comes the powdered sugar and cocoa powder. Add this in slowly and be sure the mixer is on low speed so the powdered sugar doesn't go flying all over your kitchen.
  4. Add a tablespoon of heavy cream at a time until the frosting is the consistency that you'd like it.
  5. Be sure not to beat the frosting too much. It will whip more air into your frosting.
